Basic Information

Hi! I'm Zahraa. I'm 14 years old and in this website you'll know more about me!

I'm from Bangladesh and I came to Canada 3 years ago on July 6, 2018. I'm very introverted however, once I know the person, I'll talk more than you can think. Studying takes up 70% of my life. I have to study at least 2 hours everyday. This year I have enrolled into the IB program and got to know many new teachers and classmates. I made friends and overall had a very interesting year with all the differences everywhere.

This is some general information about me. You'll be able to find out more in the following pages linked.

How I'm Like

MBTI: ISFJ (Introverted, Sensing, Feeling and Judging).

My personality is quite complicated. I am an introvert for sure however, I can't think of being alone. I really like talking to people and texting friends everyday however, I'm quite shy. Dedication and success matter a lot to me. I'm very QUITE. I exagerate a lot (when I'm with my close ones). I'm very funny. I panick a lot about my studies to be honest. Classy and simple are two huge words to describe me. I push my self out of my comfort zone only when it's necessary. I like to force myself a lot. I am VERY emotional yet, I can be very logical.

That was a short decription about my personality.
